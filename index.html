What is Battery Swapping?

Battery swapping allows EV owners to replace discharged batteries with fully charged ones in minutes. It's efficient and ideal for fleets and city commutes.

Speed

Swap your battery in under 5 minutes. No more long charging waits.

Convenience

No need for home charging setup. Visit a swap station and go.

Eco-Friendly

Promotes reuse and recycling of batteries.
